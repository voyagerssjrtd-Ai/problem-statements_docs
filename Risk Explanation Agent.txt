Capital Markets Portfolio Risk Explanation Agent

Problem Statement:
Portfolio managers need clear explanations of risk factors affecting their investments to make informed decisions.

Steps:

Input portfolio holdings and market data.

Agent analyzes risk exposures and explains in plain language.

Agent suggests risk mitigation strategies.

Agent answers follow-up questions.

Data Requirements:
Portfolio data, market risk indicators. Sample: Holdings CSV, volatility indices.

Expected Output:
Risk explanation report with mitigation advice.
